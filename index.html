TYRION LANNISTER

is a complex, witty dwarf from George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire (and HBO's Game of Thrones), known as "The Imp," who uses his sharp intellect to navigate a world that scorns him for his stature, becoming a key political player, advisor (Hand of the King), and moral compass, despite facing constant family disdain (especially from his father Tywin and sister Cersei) and enduring immense personal tragedy, ultimately finding a role advising Daenerys Targaryen and later King Bran.
